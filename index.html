Call for papers

    We invite submissions of research papers describing high-quality work conducted by members of the Computer Science Department at UC Santa Barbara. Submissions can be based on research already published, research that is to be published, or work-in-progress as long as it is created by the author(s). We encourage submissions that can be understood and appreciated by a general Computer Science audience (graduate level).


Formatting guidelines:

    Papers are limited to 2 pages and must be submitted as PDFs following the IEEE 2-column format. The name of the authors should be included (single-blind). Submissions that do not follow these guidelines risk rejection without consideration.

Presentations:

    The two best papers on each track will be selected for a long (20-minute) presentation. Other four papers on each area will be selected for short (7-minute) presentations.

Evaluation Criteria:

    Submissions will be reviewed by members of the GSWC program committee (both inside and outside the subject area of the paper) and will be judged based on their novelty, technical quality, impact, and clarity.
